Tacos

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef or chicken
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • Taco shells
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Grated cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef/chicken in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Try not to eat it straight from the pan!
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until soft and fragrant (ooooh fancy).
  3. Stir in taco seasoning and a bit of water. Simmer for 5 minutes (the smell should attract your neighbours by now).
  4. Warm up your taco shells, obviously
  5. Fill each taco shell with the beef/chicken mixture, and top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
  6. Eat as fast as possible while trying to avoid serious mouth burning
