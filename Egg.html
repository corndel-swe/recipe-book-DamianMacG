Boil 'n Egg

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 egg (or more, if you're in the mood...)
  • Water (trust me, you need it to boil the egg)
  • A pinch of salt (optional, for a touch of class)

Instructions

  1. Place the egg gently into a pot of water. Be careful — eggs don’t like surprises.
  2. Bring the water to a boil. (Hence the term boiled)
  3. Once boiling, reduce heat to a simmer and let it cook for 6 minutes. This is crucial for the perfect soft center (no pressure).
  4. Remove the egg and plunge it into ice water. It’s dramatic, but it gets the job done.
  5. After it cools, put it in an egg cup.
  6. Devour da egg.
