Pancakes

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Optional toppings: syrup, berries, whipped cream

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Please don't make a mess...
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together buttermilk, egg, and melted buttuh.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir just until smooth and silky.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set.
  6. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown. Stack 'em up!
  7. Top with your favorite toppings and let's goooooooooo!!!!!.
