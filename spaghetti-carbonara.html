Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs with grated parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with pancetta, then stir in the egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and enjoy!
