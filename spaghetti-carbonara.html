Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the sperrrghetti.
  2. Fry the pancetta until tres crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs with grated parmesan cheese in a bowl - yum yum.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with pancetta, then stir in the egg mixture (Nearly there guys).
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and bring it to your room so nobody else can have any.
